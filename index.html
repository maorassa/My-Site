By: Admin 234 123

i-Style is an algorithm powered personal styling service, which generates complete outfits for the user and high relevance shopping offers. The current online fashion shoppers are seeking customized, a personal advice when shopping. i-Style brings forward a new level of customization that is available on demand. By utilizing a unique styling algorithm, i-Style is creating personal advisor experience that is based on the intimate experience of personal styling. The concept is to provide a synergic platform to support the core needs of fashion shoppers: What am I wearing: Consumers are seeking good personalized advice that will create a positive experience that is not overwhelming the user. What else do I need: Consumers need the shopping experience to be fun and intimate. Currently, the number of offers is enormous and the consumer is working rather than enjoying a fun process of discovering offers that will answer her specific needs. What am I packing: This is the moment were the need of good personalized styling advice is highly relevant. i-Style utilizes the styling algorithm to offer a synergic platform that creates added value for the user by solving the frequent uncertainties of packing essentials.
